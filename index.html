Frequently Asked Questions About Custom T-Shirts

What materials are used for the custom T-shirts?

+

We use a variety of materials including cotton, polyester, and blended fabrics to ensure the highest quality and comfort for your custom T-shirts.

How long does it take to receive my custom T-shirts?

+

Orders are typically processed and delivered within 7-10 business days, depending on the complexity of the design and your location.

Can I upload my own design?

+

Yes, we provide an easy-to-use design tool that allows you to upload your own designs or create custom artwork using our templates and design options.

Is there a minimum order requirement?

No, we offer flexibility in order sizes. Whether you want to order just one custom T-shirt or bulk orders for an event, we've got you covered!
